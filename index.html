Guide:
What is this?

This is a browser-based virtual piano with MIDI input, visual scale/mode highlighting, and an option to lock notes outside the selected scale.

All sound is synthesized in your browser via the Web Audio API. Just JavaScript.

How do I use it?
  • Click keys or plug in your MIDI device and play. The indicator will update if your device is detected.
  • Turn on Scale hint to see keys in the selected scale highlighted visually.
  • Turn on Strict mode to disable keys that are not included in the selected scale.
  • Press the s key or silence button to quiet all sound being produced by the synth.
  • Adjust Gain to lower the output volume. This will be useful if you are playing with sound from other browser tabs.
What's next?
  • Add composition tools, like numbered hotkeys for chords built on the degrees of a selected scale, for exploring progressions easily.
  • Write a good electric piano patch.
